AIM Integrated Water Solutions Pvt.Ltd.

Founded in 2004, AIM Integrated Water Solutions Pvt.Ltd. has grown from a humble beginning into one of the leading manufacturers of water and wastewater treatment systems in India and abroad. We specialize in designing, engineering, manufacturing, and commissioning a complete range of water treatment plants and systems that cater to diverse requirements. Backed by a team of highly experienced professionals in the water treatment industry, we ensure excellence at every stage — from analyzing client needs to delivering advanced, reliable, and cost-effective solutions. With world-class infrastructure and a strong commitment to quality, we strive to provide sustainable technologies that safeguard water resources and support a healthier future.

Our Mission

Reduce, Recover & Reuse water resources.
